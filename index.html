  • Ryan is a really great school!!

    Welcome to the Ryan International Group of Institutions, India’s leading K-12 chain of schools. We’re a group of schools dedicated to encouraging students to be themselves and explore beyond boundaries. We teach your children to think, explore, and learn with joy. Our first school in Mumbai was started in 1976, and since then Ryan International Group has grown and branched over a span of 43 years. Our core interests have since then developed but have remained in the sphere of quality education, commitment to excellence and shaping the future. Ryan International Group has established friendship institutions across the globe with the mission of taking its quality of education international. Altogether, we have 135 institutions spread across India and abroad.

  • Know more about My brilliant school!!

    To stimulate students’ interest in World Affairs, Arts & Culture and Media we provide hands-on experience of the concepts learnt in class. Started in 2000, Ryan’s Indian Model United Nations is India’s first and largest United Nations simulation program, involving over 1000 young delegates from over 90 schools across Asia-Pacific. RYAN TV, the Group’s Media House, has been a pioneer in introducing the concept of Education Through the Lens. RYAN TV trains students in Journalism, Short Film Making, Advertising, Script Writing and more. Ryan Schools hosted the largest ever Regional Round (Mumbai) in the history of World Scholar’s Cup. World Scholar’s Cup is an academic decathlon challenging young minds, culminating in the Tournament of Champions held at Yale University.
